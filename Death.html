Death Note

Death Note (stylized as DEATH NOTE) is a Japanesse manga series written by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata

Plot

In Tokyio, a disaffected high-school student named Light Yagami finds the "Death Note", a mysterious black notebook wich can kill anyone as long as the user knows both the target's name and face. Initially terrified of its god-like power, Light considers the possibilities of the Death Note's abilities and kills high-profile Japanese criminals, then targeting international criminals.

Anime

The Death Note anime, directed by Tetsuro Araki and animated by Madhouse, began airing in Japan on OCtober 3, 2006, and finished its run on JUne 26,2007, totaling 37 twenty-minute episodes.
